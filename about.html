About Us

At Palcae Facility Services, we are committed to enhancing the lives of elderly people and parents without children across Kerala. Serving Pala, the entire Kottayam district, Changanacherry, Thiruvalla, and Pathanamthitta, our mission is to deliver compassionate care, companionship, and support. Our offerings include hospital caretakers, home delivery(Currently in pala) ,personal drivers, and tailored leisure trips, ensuring a comfortable and fulfilling lifestyle for seniors. We understand the unique needs of the elderly and strive to provide services that prioritize their well-being and independence. Our experienced caregivers and professional drivers are dedicated to delivering reliable and quality assistance, making us a trusted choice for elderly support in Kerala. With "Care, Comfort, Companionship" as our guiding principles, we aim to be a dependable partner for families seeking the best for their loved ones.
